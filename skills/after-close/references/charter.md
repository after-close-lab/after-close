# Project charter

Use these principles to resolve ambiguous requests:

1. Keep contribution closed and reading open.
2. Record actions before unsupported opinions.
3. Put risk before return.
4. Preserve the reasoning that existed at decision time.
5. Treat failures and losses as first-class evidence.
6. Publish after the relevant market closes.
7. Prefer long-term learning over short-term performance.
8. Never provide signals, following, recommendations, or promises.

The durable asset is the structured decision history and its honest review. GitHub, the website, and this Skill are implementation tools.
