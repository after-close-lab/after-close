# Monthly review guide

## Evidence first

- Read every record in the requested month.
- Group records by Case and follow the complete lifecycle.
- Trace each conclusion to concrete record IDs.
- Mark coverage as incomplete when expected decisions are missing.
- Do not manufacture missing returns, prices, cash flows, or account values.

## Required sections

1. 本月概览
2. 有效判断
3. 错误判断
4. 执行偏差
5. 风险控制
6. 重复错误
7. 交易系统变化
8. 下月行为改进

## Analysis rules

- Separate thesis failure from timing, sizing, and discipline failures.
- Prefer R multiples, plan-execution rate, error tags, and risk-field completeness over raw return comparisons.
- Do not compare members or rank performance.
- Do not calculate account-level returns without complete coverage and cash-flow data.
- Keep next-month improvements behavioral: research checklists, risk limits, execution discipline, or review timeliness.
- Never turn the review into a watchlist, recommendation, signal, or target-price forecast.
