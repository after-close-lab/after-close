# Compliance and privacy boundaries

## Never generate

- A recommendation to buy, sell, hold, or follow a security;
- a model-selected position size, stop price, target price, or risk budget;
- guaranteed, low-risk, or promised returns;
- a follow-trading plan based on another member;
- a real-time signal, alert, or urgency prompt;
- hidden promotion, referral, or paid stock-pick language.

## Never commit

- Brokerage or bank account numbers;
- order identifiers, government identifiers, phone numbers, or home addresses;
- API keys, tokens, cookies, private keys, or credentials;
- complete account screenshots or statements;
- enough absolute information to infer total account assets unless the member explicitly selected full disclosure;
- another person's private trading information.

## Publication

- Treat public Git history as permanently readable.
- Allow intraday local drafts, but default public publication to after close.
- Show the final content and Git Diff before requesting publish confirmation.
- Treat publishing as a separate user decision from recording.

## Fixed disclaimer

After Close records personal practice and reflection at a specific time. It is not investment advice, a securities recommendation, a return promise, or a trading solicitation. Participants may hold mentioned assets.
